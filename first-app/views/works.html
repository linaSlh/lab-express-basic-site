0 years :
Guts was found shortly after his birth by Gambino's wife, Sis, lying in a pool of mud and blood beneath the corpse of his mother hanging from a tree.
3 years :
At the age of 3, Sis died of an illness. His adoptive father, Gambino, blames him for this and holds a lifelong grudge.
6 years :
At the age of 6, he helped his father in battle.Guts is raised by Gambino as a soldier and learns how to handle weapons. He received the scar on his nose during training.
9 years :
At the age of 9, he took part in his first battle as a mercenary. During a battle, Gambino was wounded and became crippled.
11 years :
Guts was 11 years old at the time. One night, Gambino tried to kill him when he was drunk, and Guts mortally wounded him in self-defence. Surprised by the men of the troop and unable to explain himself, he has to flee.
15 years :
At 15, Guts is an experienced mercenary who travels from battlefield to battlefield. Following an attack on a castle, he meets Griffith and the Falcon troop. He becomes a member after a duel with Griffith.
18 years :
At 18, he was commander of the Falcon's shock troops. He meets Zodd the Immortal during a battle. Wounded, he and Griffith are treated in the capital. They meet the King.
20 years :
In his twenties, Guts left the Falcons. He travelled the land, training. He later joins the remnants of the Falcon troop and reunites with Griffith before the Eclipse takes place....
22 years :
1 year after finding what's left of the Falcon and Casca troupe, the Elipse takes place. Guts survives, but not without difficulty. He becomes "the Black Swordsman".
